Red Apex

Red Apex is the project of creating visual language for a conference. As the main outcomes were performed two posters and one wine bottle label.

The main theme of the conference is the discussion about drunk driving behavior. Some winemakers solidly sure that a couple of wine glasses can noticebly enchance driving skills. To prove that they decided to invite scientists, polititians and even racers to prove their statment in the final race.

Drunk VS sober.
